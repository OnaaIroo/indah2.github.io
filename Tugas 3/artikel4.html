When to Visit the Emergency Room vs. a Clinic

Emergency Room (ER): Go If You Experience:
  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe bleeding
  • Sudden confusion or fainting
  • High fever in infants
  • Broken bones or trauma
Visit the Clinic If You Have:
  • Mild fever or flu
  • Cough or sore throat
  • Rashes or skin conditions
  • Minor injuries (scrapes, bruises)
  • Ongoing health monitoring
Why This Matters:

Using the ER appropriately helps reduce overcrowding and ensures critical patients get care faster. Always call ahead if unsure.
