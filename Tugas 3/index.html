About Us

Akso Hospital is a healthcare center committed to providing high-quality, comprehensive medical services. With a team of skilled professionals and advanced technology, we are here to deliver fast, safe, and reliable care to every patient.

Our mission is to improve the quality of life for our community through accessible healthcare and personalized treatment.

Our Services

Explore the wide range of services we provide, designed to cater to various healthcare needs.

Maternity & Childcare Services

We offer dedicated services for expecting mothers and children with comprehensive support throughout every stage.

Prenatal & Postnatal Care

Monitoring health during and after pregnancy, including ultrasound scans and nutritional counseling.

Pediatric Services

Child-focused care from newborns to adolescents, including immunizations, growth monitoring, and general check-ups.

Labor & Delivery

Safe and comfortable delivery services with professional obstetricians and modern facilities.

Diagnostic & Imaging Center

Advanced diagnostic tools to support accurate and early detection of diseases.

Laboratory Tests

Blood tests, urine analysis, and other essential lab work to help diagnose various health conditions.

Radiology Services

Including X-rays, CT scans, and ultrasounds, all handled by certified radiologists.

Cardiac Diagnostics

ECG, treadmill test, and echocardiogram for early detection of heart problems.

Rehabilitation & Wellness

Focused care for recovery, long-term management, and overall well-being.

Physical Therapy

Tailored exercise programs for injury recovery, mobility improvement, and pain management.

Nutrition Counseling

Diet and lifestyle plans for patients with chronic illnesses or those aiming for better health.

Mental Health Support

Counseling and psychiatric services for emotional well-being and psychological conditions.

Our Facilities

Experience modern medical facilities designed for comfort, safety, and comprehensive care.

Private Patient Rooms

Spacious and clean rooms ranging from standard to VIP class, complete with amenities like AC, private bathrooms, and guest sofas.

24/7 Emergency Unit

Always ready to respond to medical emergencies with trained professionals and life-saving equipment.

Modern Operating Theaters

Equipped with the latest surgical tools and maintained under strict hygiene standards to support safe procedures.

Laboratory & Diagnostic Center

On-site lab and imaging services, including X-ray, ultrasound, and blood tests, for fast and accurate diagnostics.

Pharmacy Services

In-house pharmacy with a wide range of medicines, ensuring quick access to prescriptions and trusted brands.

Prayer Room & Cafeteria

Comfortable spaces for spiritual needs and healthy meals, available for both patients and visitors.
